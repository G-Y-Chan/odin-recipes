Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.
You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below,
but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • White sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the order below:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat
  5. Cover with foil and bake
    The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
    Cover with foil for the firest 25 minutes, then let it cookj uncovered for the final 25 minutes.
  6. Let the lasagna rest at room temperature for 15 minutes before serving
