Pancake

Description

Ready for the best pancakes ever?
This easy recipe uses basic ingredients for a stack of golden, fluffy pancakes.
With a simple process, you'll have warm and delicious pancakes in no time.
A perfect, easy, and tasty breakfast to brighten your morning!

Ingredients

  • 250g plain flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 egg
  • 250ml buttermilk

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make a hole in the middle and add the egg.
  3. Mix them quickly after breaking the yolk and pouring in the buttermilk until they become stick batter.
  4. Do not beat when mixing as gluten will appear in the flour, which prevents the pancakes from rising.
  5. Fry the misture in hot griddle pan and serve when it is still hot.
